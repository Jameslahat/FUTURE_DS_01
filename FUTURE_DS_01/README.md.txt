## Key Insights
- Sales show strong seasonal trends, with peaks during year-end months.
- The West region contributes the highest overall sales.
- A small number of products generate a large portion of total revenue.
- Technology and Furniture categories drive most of the sales.
- Certain states significantly outperform others, indicating regional market concentration.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing regions like the West.
- Focus inventory and promotions on top-selling products.
- Develop targeted strategies to improve sales in underperforming regions.
- Plan inventory and campaigns ahead of seasonal demand spikes.
