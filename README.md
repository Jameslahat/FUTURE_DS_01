# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover revenue trends, top-performing products, high-value categories, and regional sales performance.  
The objective is to generate meaningful business insights and actionable recommendations that support data-driven decision-making.

This project was completed as **Task 1** under the **Data Science & Analytics Internship** at :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}.

---

## Business Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify monthly sales trends
- Determine top-selling products
- Evaluate category-wise sales contribution
- Analyze regional and state-wise performance
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Dataset Information
- Dataset Name: Superstore Sales Dataset  
- File Used: `train.csv`  
- Data Type: Public retail sales data  
- Key Columns:
  - Order Date
  - Sales
  - Category
  - Sub-Category
  - Product Name
  - Region
  - State
  - Quantity
  - Profit

##  Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Top 10 products by total sales

- Region-wise sales performance
- State-level sales analysis

---

##  Key Insights
- Sales exhibit clear seasonal trends, with peaks during year-end months.
- The West region generates the highest overall sales.
- A small number of products contribute a large share of total revenue.
- Technology and Furniture categories dominate sales performance.
- Certain states significantly outperform others, indicating regional concentration.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing regions such as the West.
- Focus promotions and inventory planning on top-selling products.
- Develop targeted strategies to improve sales in underperforming regions.
- Prepare inventory in advance to meet seasonal demand spikes.
